Our Story

2 December 2025
Before our story officially began, we were just two friends spending a few weeks together, brought together by me coming back to Asia Syndicate because of Ryme, farming Batu Purple and Biru untuk craft the M4 Joker at MIRP. Somewhere between those simple conversations, random jokes, and late-night sessions, something slowly started to grow without us even realizing it. Just a few days before everything changed, we randomly decided to play Schedule 1 together, just the two of us, as friends. Neither of us knew that those small moments would quietly lead us to the day we finally became more than friends. And on 2 December 2025, what started as friendship turned into something much more meaningful. The beginning of us.

25 December 2025, Our First Date
That night felt like time slowed down just for us. You had just arrived from your hometown, Kedah, on your way back to campus in Bintulu, Sarawak, landing at the airport around 11PM, and from that moment until 5:30AM, every minute became a memory I will always treasure. We started with something simple: McDonald’s. You had the Prosperity Burger medium set with curly fries and a bottle of water, while I took the large set with normal fries and Coke Zero. I even offered you some of my fries, but you smiled and said you didn’t want normal fries, a small moment that somehow made the night feel even more personal and memorable. Sitting together on the bench at the KLIA1 viewing area, we shared our meal while quietly watching airplanes pass by, talking about everything and nothing at the same time. We spent the night walking around the airport again and again, exploring every corner like two people who never wanted the night to end. At 3AM, we stopped by the Turkish Delight stall to try cream puffs, laughing at how random yet perfect the moment felt. We took pictures at the Monopoly set, capturing small pieces of a night that already felt unforgettable. Later, around 4:30AM, we sat together at McDonald’s again, sharing an ice cream cone, staying close and quietly wishing the morning would arrive just a little slower. And when 5:30AM finally came, the moment I had to leave. I remember how heavy that step away from you felt. Part of me wanted to turn back, return to the airport, and stay just a little longer, because leaving you that morning was harder than I expected. But I had to go home to prepare for my 9AM FYP meeting with my supervisor, carrying with me the quiet sadness of saying goodbye, and at the same time the warm feeling of knowing that the night we just spent together was something truly special, a memory I would replay again and again.

30 January 2026 — Our Little Engagement
On 30 January 2026 at around 2AM, something unexpected and unforgettable happened. We got randomly engaged on MIRP. It was completely a spur-of-the-moment decision, something none of us planned, and it all started because of Tupai. What began as a simple late-night hangout session quickly turned into one of the most special memories I will always hold close. Thankfully, all of my ooc friends were able to be there to witness it. Rexsz, Opies, Tupai, and Skykid were all present, making the moment feel even more meaningful. Uncle Alexander had inved everyone who was available at the time, and seeing everyone gather there made the moment feel surprisingly warm and real. It might have been spontaneous, simple, and a little chaotic, but it was ours. And that is what makes it so memorable to me. That night reminded me that sometimes the most beautiful memories are not the ones carefully planned, but the ones that happen naturally, with the right people around us.

15 March 2026 — Our Next Chapter
Even though it is the fasting month and we may not be able to do much, spending the afternoon together from 1PM to 6:30PM already means more than enough to me. Sometimes the most beautiful moments are not the busiest ones, but the quiet hours where we simply sit side by side, talking, laughing, and watching airplanes pass slowly across the sky. It may just be a simple day of chilling, hanging out, and airplane watching, but moments like these are the ones that quietly build our story; peaceful, sincere, and filled with the comfort of being together.

28 March 2026 — More Moments Together
Before you head back to Bintulu, we will spend another special day together, our little “NakNak Date” at KLIA2. I will be bringing brownies for you to enjoy, something small but prepared with the thought of seeing you smile. That day, I will show you how to use the train around the airport, and we will explore the place together just like we always do, turning simple moments into memories. We will sit down, enjoy our meal, and you will finally get to taste the spicy NakNak flavour that once made me sick, a small story that somehow became part of our shared memories too. It may seem like just another day before your flight back, but for me it is another moment I know I will hold close, because every time we meet, even the simplest plans become something special.

And this is only the beginning. Every day with you is another page, another memory, another reason I’m grateful that our paths met that day.

Love Letter

Dear Jasmine,

Today is Valentine’s Day, and I keep thinking about how everything between us began. From the days when we were just friends spending time together playing games, talking about random things, and laughing at our own jokes, to the moment we slowly realized that what we had was becoming something more. None of it felt rushed, none of it felt forced. It simply happened naturally, and somehow that makes everything feel even more special to me.

I still remember the first time we met at the airport, how nervous I felt walking toward you, wondering what I should say and how I should act. Yet the moment I saw you, all those nervous thoughts faded, and I felt something calm and warm inside me. That night, sitting together, watching airplanes, sharing food, walking around the airport with no real destination, and talking about everything and nothing at the same time became one of the most unforgettable memories I have. Even now, when I think back to it, I still feel the same happiness I felt that night.

Some of my favorite moments with you are not always the big ones. Sometimes they are the small and quiet moments, the times when we joke around, tease each other, share random thoughts late at night, or simply sit together without needing anything special to happen. Even the distance between us does not take those moments away. Every message you send, every call we share, and every time we spend together online still makes me feel close to you.

I know that life can be busy, classes can be tiring, and sometimes days do not go the way we want them to. But even on those days, I want you to know that I am always here, ready to listen, ready to talk, and ready to stay by your side through whatever comes. I may not always be perfect, and I may not always say the right things, but my intention has always been the same, which is to care for you, support you, and continue choosing you every single day.

I look forward to every plan we make together, every future date, every movie night, every game session, every airport meeting, and every small moment that we will slowly turn into memories. I like making plans ahead of time because it gives me something to look forward to, something that reminds me that more beautiful days with you are always coming.

Thank you for your patience, for your understanding, for your laughter, and for the warmth you bring into my life. Thank you for staying even during moments that were not easy, for choosing to grow together instead of walking away, and for allowing our story to continue becoming stronger with time.

This Valentine’s Day is only one day, but what I feel for you is something I carry every day. I want to keep building memories with you, keep laughing with you, keep growing with you, and keep writing our story together, month after month, year after year. No matter how much time passes, I hope we will always be able to look back at the beginning and smile, knowing that everything we built started from two people who simply enjoyed spending time together.

Happy Valentine’s Day, Jasmine. Thank you for being part of my life, and thank you for being someone I can love, care for, and look forward to seeing again and again.

Always yours,
Khairul ❤️
