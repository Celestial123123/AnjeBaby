One month ago, everything that felt ordinary suddenly became something I looked forward to every single day, because of you. I still remember our first night at KLIA so clearly. But somehow, it became one of the most meaningful nights of my life. That night, I realized it wasnt about where we were or what we were doing. It was about who I was with. Being beside you made everything feel calm, warm, and right. Even after you left, the memory stayed with me. It gave me something to hold onto, something to look forward to. You became someone I wanted to talk to every day, someone I wanted to share everything with. Our first month wasnt about grand moments. It was about discovering each other, slowly, sincerely, and honestly. And in that short time, you became someone very special to me.

By the second month, you were no longer just someone important to me. You had become part of my daily life, part of my routine, and part of my happiness. We shared more conversations, more laughter, and even moments where things werent perfect. But through everything, we stayed. We listened. We understood. We chose each other. The night we got engaged, even though it was spontaneous and unexpected, meant more to me than I could explain. It wasnt about the setting or the timing. It was about us choosing each other, even in the most random moment. You brought comfort into my life in a way I never experienced before. Talking to you became the best part of my day. This month showed me that what we have is real, and worth protecting.

Month 3 — You Became My Home ▶

Three months ago, you were someone new in my life. Now, you are someone I cannot imagine my days without. Being with you feels natural. Comfortable. Safe. Even simple moments, sitting together, talking, or quietly watching tiktoks, mean everything to me. Its not about doing something extraordinary. Its about being beside you. You have seen my good days and my difficult ones, and you stayed. That means more to me than words can explain. Somewhere along the way, without realizing it, you stopped being just someone I loved. You became my home.

To You, My Love

Three months ago, I didnt know that someone could slowly become part of my everyday life in ways I never expected. You didnt just become someone I love. You became the person behind so many of my small, quiet efforts. The reason I log in, the reason I stay up, and the reason ordinary things feel meaningful.

In Heartopia, collecting berries, doing dailies, and finishing quests never felt like chores to me. I did them happily, because I knew they were for you. Because somewhere in those small tasks, it felt like I was taking care of you in the only ways I could from where I am. Every berry, every quest, every little progress we made together meant something, not because of the game, but because it was with you.

And in Minecraft, we didnt just build structures. We built pieces of our world together. Our cherry blossom house, quiet and peaceful, feels like a place where time slows down. The house in the wall, the waterfall, the wizard tower, the library, and the potion room. Each one exists because I wanted to create something beautiful in a world where you exist beside me.

The fisherman house, the pet house for our parrots, cats, and dogs, and even the two baby happy ghasts we will be raising, they all feel like small parts of a life we are building together.

But my favorite of all might be the pond.

Two axolotls.
Blue and yellow.
Jasmine and Khairul.

Swimming side by side, together in the same quiet space. Even though there was an accident, and Khairul went missing, but we finally found him again. It may just be a small thing in a game, but to me, it means everything.

And even in Valorant, no matter how the matches go, no matter the wins or losses, Im always happy just being there with you. It was never about rank. It was never about performance. It was always about sharing those moments with you. Even if we lose every game, I would still choose to queue with you again and again, because being beside you is what makes it worth it.

These past three months arent just memories to me. They are proof that love can exist in late nights, in quiet effort, in shared worlds, and in the small things people might not notice.

You became part of my routines.
Part of my effort.
Part of my peace.

And I want you to know, every small thing I do with you, and for you, comes from a place that is real and sincere.

Happy 3 months, my love.

And if I had to start over from the beginning, I would still choose you.

Again, and again, and again. ❤️

Forever us 💕
